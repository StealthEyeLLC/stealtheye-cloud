# Replay Proof Surface

## Purpose

The Replay Proof Surface turns browser exploration and visual observations into repeatable proof artifacts.

## S2 scope

S2 creates the replay contract and artifact surface. It does not yet build a full visual viewer.

## Contract

S2 introduces:

1. `BrowserReplayPackV0`
2. `BrowserArtifactIndexV0`
3. deterministic browser fixture proof
4. artifact index validation

## Future expansion

S3+ may convert exploratory browser sessions into deterministic replay packs and public proof summaries.

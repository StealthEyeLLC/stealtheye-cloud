# Deep Research Packets

## Purpose

Deep Research packets represent research tasks that require user-started Deep Research or equivalent sourced research workflows.

## S3 contracts

1. `DeepResearchTaskPacketV0`
2. `ResearchResultImportV0`

## Rules

1. A task packet is a request, not proof that Deep Research ran.
2. Results must be imported through `ResearchResultImportV0`.
3. Research outputs need citations and summary boundaries.
4. StealthEye Cloud must not claim it silently launched Deep Research.

## S3 status

S3 creates the representation contracts and docs only. User-started mode launch remains outside hidden automation.

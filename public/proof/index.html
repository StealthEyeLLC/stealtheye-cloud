StealthEye Cloud Proof Canvas

Public-safe proof summary surface for missions, CI, browser artifacts, Relay, Seal, workers, and decisions.

Mission

Mission state comes from STEALTHEYE_ACTIVE.md.

CI

Proof status comes from GitHub Actions.

Browser

Browser artifacts come from proof-browser.

Seal

Checkpoint truth comes from STEALTHEYE_SEAL.json.

Relay

Continuation context comes from STEALTHEYE_RELAY.md and STEALTHEYE_RELAY.json.

Worker

Worker result imports are represented by packet contracts.

Decision

Frozen decisions come from STEALTHEYE_DECISIONS.md.
